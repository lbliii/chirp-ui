# Typography effects

Marketing and hero typography: `gradient_text.html`, `glitch_text.html`, `neon_text.html`, `typewriter.html`, `text_reveal.html`, `marquee.html`, `number_ticker.html`, etc.

## Variants

Registry keys include `typewriter`, `glitch`, `neon`, `text-reveal`, `marquee`, `number-ticker`.

## Motion

Use `--chirpui-duration-*` and `--chirpui-easing-*` tokens — not raw seconds in templates — see [Motion and transitions](../theming/motion-and-transitions.md).
